Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese dish that consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a flavorful broth, often garnished with various toppings such as sliced pork, eggs, seaweed, and vegetables. It has gained popularity worldwide and comes in a variety of regional styles, each with its own unique broth and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Broth (chicken or veg)
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Veggies like carrots or kale
  • toppings like some panko, egg, chili oil

Preparation steps

  1. Stir fry the garlic and ginger
  2. add chicken broth and shittake mushrooms
  3. add noodles and cook the noodles in the broth
  4. add any vegetables such as kale or carrots, cook until tender
  5. top it with an egg or chilli oil or whatever else you would like to add
  6. serve and enjoy!
