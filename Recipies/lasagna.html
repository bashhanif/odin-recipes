Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delectable Italian dish that is a harmonious symphony of flavors and textures. Layered with precision, it combines tender sheets of wide pasta with rich and savory components such as succulent meat, creamy b√©chamel sauce, and tangy tomato sauce. The dish is an embodiment of comfort, its layers melding together in the oven to create a masterpiece of indulgence. The top layer is often adorned with a blanket of melted cheese, achieving a golden, bubbling crust that's irresistibly enticing. With each forkful, one experiences a delightful interplay of hearty ingredients, making lasagna a timeless and satisfying culinary experience.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh basil
  • Hot Italian sausage

Preperation Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of bolognese on the bottom. Make a single layer of noodles, then add more meat sauce.
  2. Then, add a couple handfuls of mozzarella and parmesan, making sure to sprinkle it to the edges
  3. Add another layer of noodles on top of the cheese.
  4. Continue layering until you have used up all of the ingredients.
  5. Bake until warmed all the way through. The cheese should be browned and bubbly
  6. Let the lasagna rest so it can firm up before slicing.
