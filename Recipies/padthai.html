Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a popular Thai dish that consists of stir-fried rice noodles, typically with ingredients like shrimp, chicken, tofu, egg, and a combination of vegetables. The dish is flavored with a tangy and slightly sweet sauce made from ingredients like tamarind, fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. It's often garnished with chopped peanuts, lime wedges, and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Protein
  • Pad Thai sauce
  • eggs
  • tofu
  • bean sprout
  • chives
  • peanuts
  • turnip

Preparation steps

  1. Prepare the noodles. Simmer or soak the noodles just until tender and pliable.
  2. Make the sauce.
  3. Prep the ingredients. Chop your ingredients and set them near the stove.
  4. Cook the protein. Sear your protein of choice until it is cooked on the outside, but not cooked all the way through.
  5. Scramble the egg
  6. Add noodles.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
